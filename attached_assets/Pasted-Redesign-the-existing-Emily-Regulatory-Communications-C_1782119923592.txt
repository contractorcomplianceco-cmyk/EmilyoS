The app must still help her track agency communications, regulatory matters, board follow-ups, DOT / Revenue / EPA / licensing agency communications, deficiencies, deadlines, escalations, tasks, applications, chats, and items needing Rose review.

But visually, make it feel much more personal to Emily.
