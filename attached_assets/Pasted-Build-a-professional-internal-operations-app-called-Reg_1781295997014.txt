Build a professional internal operations app called Regulatory Communications Command Center.

This app is for Emily Jones, Director of Compliance and Regulatory Communications. Emily leads the company’s communication and follow-up with government agencies, regulatory departments, licensing boards, Departments of Revenue, Departments of Transportation, Environmental Protection Agencies, Secretary of State offices, tax agencies, permitting offices, and any other compliance-related authority.

The app should function as Emily’s command center for tracking agency communications, regulatory matters, deadlines, deficiencies, follow-ups, escalations, and agency knowledge.

Primary user:
Emily Jones

Secondary users:
Rose / leadership, compliance staff, account managers, and future members of Emily’s department.

Build the app as an internal operational dashboard with the following sections:

1. Dashboard
Show high-level visibility into:
- Open regulatory matters
- Overdue follow-ups

- High-risk matters
- Agency delays
- Open deficiencies or agency requests
- Escalations needing review
- Matters waiting on client
- Matters waiting on internal team
- Team workload

Important design requirements:
- The app should feel like a clean professional internal command center, not a marketing website.
- Use a left sidebar navigation.
- Use clear dashboard cards, tables, filters, badges, and detail drawers or modals.
- Make the interface dense enough for operations work but still easy to read.
- Use professional colors, not playful or overly bright styling.
- Do not use decorative landing-page sections.
- Build the usable app as the first screen.
- Include realistic sample data for testing.
- Make add/edit forms work for agencies, matters, communications, deficiencies, escalations, and knowledge entries.
- Make table filters and search functional.
- Make status/risk badges visually clear.
- Include empty states.
- Include a role boundary reminder that Emily tracks communication, follow-up, documentation, and routing, but does not provide legal advice, final compliance opinions, tax advice, pricing decisions, refund approvals, contract changes, or guaranteed agency outcomes.

Data persistence:
For this first build, use browser localStorage or the simplest Replit-supported persistence so edits survive refresh. Structure the data clearly so it can later become a Zoho CRM or database-backed system.

Architecture note:
This app is a prototype / workflow definition tool unless later approved as the permanent system of record. It should help define the future Zoho CRM or production database requirements for regulatory communications.

Do not add automated email sending, agency portal login automation, legal advice generation, tax advice generation, or automatic client-facing communication. All external communication should remain human-reviewed.
